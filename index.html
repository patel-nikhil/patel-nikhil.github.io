About Me

I am a software engineering student, a massive soccer fan, and I love puzzles, board games, and books. I also enjoy learning new technologies and writing new and challenging software programs. I am passionate about everything I do, whether it is academic, professional, or personal. I enjoy challenges and finding practical and creative solutions to the problems and opportunities I face in my career and life. I look forward to the adventures and opportunities that are yet to come my way.

My education has provided me with both theory as well as hands-on experience developing software projects. The Engineering and Management program also provides a background in business and marketing through a variety of financial and commerce courses.

I recently completed an internship as Junior Technical Business Analyst at NexgenRx where I was able to apply and develop my understanding of software and also database design principles. I was given responsibility to manage and undertake a variety of projects, from gathering of requirements from clients and coworkers, to development, testing and deployment of the final product.
